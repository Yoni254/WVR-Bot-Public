About Us

We are the West Virginia Republic (WVR), a Fallout 76 Player Made Faction based on Strength, Unity, Loyalty, Leadership, and Initiative or as we like to refer to it, (S.U.L.L.I). We are looking for new members to join our cause and invite you to our family as brothers & sisters in a united cause. We hope you can help us achieve a peaceful and prosperous new Appalachia, free of raiders, war hungry clans and any other threat we may run across. We here in the WVR want to personally speak to all of you and answer any questions you may have, we hope to see you soon and placed amongst fellow brothers an sisters.
